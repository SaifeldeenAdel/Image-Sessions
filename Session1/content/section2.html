Image Operations

There are so many ways to modify image arrays, we will explore some of the most common and useful ones in our work.
Starting with your normal addition and subtraction of pixel values which results in brighter and darker images.
Be careful adding to images of type uint8 as this may cause an overflow demonstrated in the notebook


Image partitioning

Sometimes you will need to crop out a certain part of your image to get rid of something.
This can easily be done using numpy indexing similar to what we did before.

Image resizing

A problem we will face when working with multiple images later is them being of different dimensions. So we need to learn how to get an image into our desired dimensions.
Additionally you might also want to resize an image to create a smaller image with the same aspect ratio

Rotation and Reflection

Built in functions in openCV let you rotate images by multiples of 90 and reflect them along the y or x axes.
