Working with Multiple Images

Mathematical Operations

In order to perform any operations with 2 or more images together, we need to ensure they have the same dimensions and

Image Concatenation

We might also want to stitch together multiple images side by side or in a grid.
So you will learn how to use resizing and indexing to create a new image from concatenating multiple images together.
